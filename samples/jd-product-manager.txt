Senior Product Manager — B2B SaaS Analytics Platform

About the role
We are hiring a Senior Product Manager to lead our enterprise analytics platform. You will own the product strategy for a suite of B2B SaaS tools used by Fortune 500 customers across retail, logistics, and financial services. You will report to the VP of Product and partner daily with a team of 15 engineers, 3 designers, and a dedicated customer success group.

Responsibilities
- Own the product roadmap for our core analytics platform; balance quarterly commitments against longer-term strategic bets
- Define and prioritize features based on customer research, usage data, and commercial impact
- Partner closely with engineering, design, and go-to-market teams from spec through launch
- Run customer discovery interviews with enterprise buyers, champions, and end-users
- Translate enterprise requirements into shippable specifications that engineers can build from without ambiguity
- Define success metrics and drive measurable business impact on activation, retention, and expansion revenue
- Present product updates to executives internally and to customer stakeholders in QBRs
- Mentor junior PMs on the team; contribute to our product-management craft guide

Requirements
- 5+ years of product management experience
- 3+ years in B2B SaaS, ideally with enterprise customers (>$100k ACV)
- Strong SQL and data-analysis skills — you should be comfortable querying production data in Snowflake or similar to answer your own questions without waiting on an analyst
- Experience shipping features that drive measurable revenue or retention impact, with clear before/after metrics
- Excellent written communication; you can write a crisp one-pager or PRD without help
- Experience running discovery interviews with technical enterprise buyers
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience

Nice to have
- Experience with Amplitude, Mixpanel, or similar product-analytics tools
- Background in Figma for lightweight mocking and design review
- Prior experience with Jira for agile delivery
- Exposure to the analytics, data infrastructure, or business-intelligence space

Benefits
- Competitive salary plus equity
- Remote-first, with quarterly offsites
- Health, dental, vision, and a generous learning budget
