Senior Machine Learning Engineer — Recommendation Systems

About the role
We are looking for a Senior ML Engineer to own the recommendation and ranking stack that powers personalization across our consumer app (15M MAU). You will drive model development end-to-end: from offline experimentation and feature engineering through online A/B evaluation and production serving at scale. You will report to our Head of ML and collaborate with platform engineers, data scientists, and product managers.

Responsibilities
- Design, train, and ship deep-learning ranking models (two-tower, transformer-based sequence models, or similar) in production
- Own the full ML lifecycle: feature engineering, offline evaluation, online A/B testing, monitoring, and retraining
- Work in PyTorch (or equivalent) for modeling and with Spark / Flink for large-scale data pipelines
- Build and maintain low-latency model-serving infrastructure (sub-100ms P99)
- Partner with data engineering to design feature stores and ensure training/serving consistency
- Contribute to our internal ML platform (experiment tracking, model registry, deployment tooling)
- Mentor mid-level ML engineers; review research proposals and model cards

Requirements
- 5+ years of hands-on machine-learning engineering experience, with at least 2 years shipping ranking, search, or recommender systems to production
- Strong Python, PyTorch (or TensorFlow), and SQL; production experience with one of Spark, Flink, or Beam
- Deep understanding of ranking models — learning-to-rank, neural retrieval, sequence modeling, calibration
- Experience running and interpreting offline evaluation (NDCG, MRR, AUC) and online A/B experiments
- Comfortable reading and implementing recent research papers in recommendation systems
- BS, MS, or PhD in Computer Science, Machine Learning, Statistics, or related quantitative field
- Experience at scale: training on millions+ of examples, serving to millions+ of users

Nice to have
- Publications at NeurIPS, ICML, KDD, RecSys, or similar venues
- Experience with JAX, Ray, or large-scale distributed training
- Familiarity with embedding-based retrieval (FAISS, ScaNN)
- Prior experience in consumer apps with strong personalization (content feeds, e-commerce, streaming)

This is a hands-on IC role. You will write code every day.
